## THE EXPERIENCE

Six chapters. Eighteen illustrations. One scroll.

Each chapter pins to the viewport and cycles through three camera angles - 3/4 front, side elevation, top-down plan — zooming deeper into the component with every scroll tick. Live spec counters fire when you hit the details. The engine synthesizer hums when you ask it to.

Film grain. Scanlines. HUD brackets. A GPS coordinate readout that jitters in real-time. This isn't a spec sheet, it's a brief.
